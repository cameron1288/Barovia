Fates & Active Quests

1
Reach the Amber Temple

Strahd told Flint: 'Amber Temple has noticed you. I know it.' Seriac's final message: 'Seek out the mountain, find the temple — let's hunt a vampire.' This is where Strahd made his original pact with the Dark Powers. It almost certainly contains the Sunsword (Tarokka Card 3: amber prison). Getting there likely requires going through Argynvostholt first.

2
Find the History/Knowledge of the Ancients (Tarokka Card 1)

The party has stumbled across the prophecy of the Card of History with Stella Wachter leading us to Leo Delinzia to the long-lost Tome of Strahd. By unearthing this ancient text, they have pierced the veil of Barovia's illusions and exposed the dark, tragic truths of the vampire lord's mortal life, his jealousy, and his blood pact with darkness. With the tyrant's deepest secrets and weaknesses now laid bare in their hands, this crucial thread of Madam Eva's fortune is officially resolved, forever changing how the companions face the master of Ravenloft.

3
Find the Sunsword (Tarokka Card 3)

The one weapon Strahd truly fears — confirmed by his own Tome: 'That cursed sword that Sergei brought! I must dispose of that awful tool!' Hidden in 'an amber prison, where the devil fears to tread.' Argynvostholt (House of the Fallen Dragon) is a required step before finding it. The Sunsword has a platinum hilt with a deep blue sapphire — a blade of pure sunlight.

4
Find the Holy Symbol of Ravenkind (Tarokka Card 2)

A powerful holy relic in a wolf's den in the hills overlooking a mountain lake. Once belonging to Mother Night (Fiona is Daughter of Mother Night), the dark deity of lycanthropes. Most likely location: hills above Lake Baratok in the northwest, where the spectral knight warned about werewolves. One of the three artifacts needed to fight Strahd.

5
Find the Tarokka Ally (Tarokka Card 4)

A female Vistana wanderer searching for her mentor — find her at Saint Markovia's Abbey in Krezk. She moves constantly. Most likely: Esmeralda Davenir, Van Richter's student, whose wagon was already at Kazan's Tower.

6
Travel to Krezk

Multiple reasons: (1) Tarokka ally at Saint Markovia's Abbey. (2) The blessed pool there has significance. (3) The Abbey itself may connect to other quests. (4) Zolt and Matilda (trappers from Krezk) are potential contacts.

7
Visit Argynvostholt

Both the spectral bridge knight and Tarokka Card 3 point here. Find Ser Godfrey Gwilym of the Order of the Silver Dragon. Argynvostholt is the 'House of the Fallen Dragon' from the Tarokka reading — a required step before the Sunsword. South of the walled town on the ash-grey path.

8
Strahd's Dinner Invitation

Rahadin delivered Strahd's invitation in Session 27: dine at Castle Ravenloft in six days, safe passage both ways promised. Strahd also offered a separate reward at Yester Hill — meet him at the base of the whispering wall after the ritual. Almost certainly a test, a trap, or both. Refusing likely also carries consequences.

9
Strahd's Counter-Reading — Beware the Traitor

Vel overheard Strahd speaking in verse that appeared to mock or subvert the Tarokka reading: 'She thinks the cards speak truth, that rivers can be dammed / The Sword lies in the hand of the Dead / The Ally walks in the Shadow / Cast a light on the Traitor and call him friend / They will trust the wrong soul, let them chase a ghost.' Someone the party trusts may betray them. The Sunsword may be with a dead person. The ally may not be trustworthy — or may be hidden. Keep this in mind at all times.

10
Recover the 3 Winery Gemstones

Three enchanted gemstones that power 'Champagne de La Stomp' were stolen: (1) An unknown raven while Erwin failed to watch. (2) Baba Lasaga's scarecrows took the Red Dragon Crush gem. (3) The Forest Folk took the third. The Keepers of the Feather need them to maintain the winery and their resistance efforts.

11
Confront Baba Lasaga

The witch in the Ruins of Berez created the flying blight creatures, stole a winery gem, and has sworn to destroy the Martikov family. Her base may also be connected to the Swamp Fane — one of Strahd's three ancient power sources.

12
The Three Fanes of Barovia

Strahd's Tome confirms he claimed power from three Fanes: Swamp, Forest, and Mountain. This is why the land itself obeys him. Restoring these could significantly weaken his power. Likely locations: Swamp Fane near Ruins of Berez?, Forest Fane near Yester Hill?, Mountain Fane unknown?

13
Retrieve Franz's Children

Franz's children Josephia and Camel were found in a bookcase at Old Bonegrinder in Session 26. Were they actually freed and returned to him? Also need to address Franz's wasting disease.

14
Investigate the Sacred Tome of Strahd

Flint collected the Deep Crimson Red Tome in Session 29. Key contents: Strahd's personal history, obsession with Tatyana, pact with Death, fear of the Sunsword, and the three Fanes. The final page has a triangle symbol inscribed 'Exechanter'.

15
Restore the Bones of St. Andral

Recovered from the coffin maker's shop and returned to Father Lucian in Session 16. Church protection restored.

16
Kill Isek

Killed at Lake Zarovich in Session 23. Loot: Silvered Battle Axe, Amber Shard, 35gp, Studded Leather.

17
Save Stella Wachter's Soul

Ethereal Plane ritual performed in Session 28. Stella entered the binding circle. Viktor confirmed she was back in her body when the party returned.

18
Defeat Morganta and the Nightmare Hags

Morganta and daughters killed in Sessions 26-27. Heartstone retrieved. Franz's children found in the mill.

19
Winery Rescue

Spiggot destroyed Wand in Session 33. Every blight froze and crumbled. Martikovs returned safely. 2000xp.
