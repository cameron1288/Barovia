Card 1: History & Knowledge

9 of Glyphs — The Traitor
"This card tells of history. Knowledge of the ancients will help you better understand your enemy. I see this ancient foe of an old and noble house. The lost soul will lead you to him."

Notes: This card points to a living or spectral person — someone fallen from a noble house, betrayed, who holds knowledge of Strahd's deepest secrets. The Traitor card and the accusatory priest suggest betrayal is central to their story. The lost soul is assumed to be Stella Wachter and the ancient foe of the old and noble house is Leo Delinzia who gave us Strahd's Tome.

Card 2: Holy Symbol of Hope

5 of Swords — The Myrmidon
"Look for the den of wolves in the hills overlooking a mountain lake. The treasure belongs to Mother Night."

Notes: A powerful holy relic lies in a wolf's den in the hills overlooking a mountain lake. It once belonged to Mother Night — the dark deity worshipped by lycanthropes in Barovia. The Holy Symbol of Ravenkind is one of the three key artifacts needed to fight Strahd. The most likely location: hills above Lake Baratok in the northwest, where the spectral bridge knight warned the party about werewolves. Fiona Wachter is known as the Daughter of Mother Night as referred to in Session 25 by a Forest Druid.

Card 3: The Sunsword

8 of Glyphs — The Bishop
"I see a weapon of light, a blade of pure sun. Hidden away in an amber prison where the devil fears to tread."

Notes: The Sunsword is the one weapon Strahd truly fears — confirmed by his own Tome: 'That cursed sword that Sergei brought! I must dispose of that awful tool! I fear and hate it as much as the sun.' Sergei brought it to Barovia as a wedding gift. After killing Sergei, Strahd tried to get rid of it. Hidden in 'an amber prison, where the devil fears to tread' — almost certainly the Amber Temple in the mountains, a place Strahd avoids. 'The House of the Fallen Dragon' is Argynvostholt — visiting there is a required step before finding the Sunblade.

Card 4: The Destined Ally

The Mists
"A Vistana wanders this land alone, searching for her mentor. She does not linger in one place for long. Seek her at Saint Markovia's Abbey."

Notes: A female Vistani wanderer, searching for her lost mentor, is the party's destined ally. She moves constantly and doesn't stay in one place long. Find her at Saint Markovia's Abbey in Krezk. This strongly suggests she is Esmeralda Davenir — Van Richter's own student, whose wagon was already at Kazan's Tower. If so, the party has crossed paths with her work without ever meeting her.

Card 5: The Enemy's Location

The Marionette
"Look to Castle Ravenloft's heights. Find the beating heart of the castle. He waits nearby."

Notes: When the time comes, Strahd will be found in Castle Ravenloft's heights — its upper reaches. The Marionette card is meaningful: even Strahd, for all his power, is pulled by unseen strings — the Dark Powers that trapped him in Barovia. He is master of nothing. Do NOT go to Ravenloft until all three artifacts are secured. Without the Sunsword especially, Strahd is effectively unkillable.
