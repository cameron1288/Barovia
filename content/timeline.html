Before Arrival — Background Events
~3 Months Before Arrival (Octyavr)

Doru's Rebellion

LoreStrahd
▼

Doru raised a rebellion against Strahd, awakening him from his sleep. Six nights of horror followed across Barovia. Doru was turned into a vampire spawn and imprisoned in the crypt beneath Father Donavich's church. Father Donavich has not left the church since.

~6 Months Before Arrival

Stella Wachter's Soul Trapped

Lore
▼

While attempting to build a teleportation circle, Stella Wachter and Viktor Vallakovich triggered a catastrophic accident. Stella's soul was ripped from her body and trapped in the Ethereal Plane. She has roughly one week before she is lost forever — a ticking clock when the party arrives in Vallaki.

~24th–26th Octyavr — Day 1 | Sessions 1–2
Sessions 1–2

Arrival Through the Mists — Daguerfort Orphanage

MilestoneTravel
▼

The party arrived through the mists and were drawn into the Daguerfort Orphanage — the Durst family home. They discovered the tragic Durst family history, bloodied letters, and an ancient dagger. They were spirited through a misty gateway into the Svalich Woods as the Harvest Festival began in Barovia village.

Sessions 1–2

⭐ Death House — Walter Slain

CombatMilestone
▼

Deep within the orphanage dungeon, the party confronted Walter — a grotesque abomination, the twisted child of nanny Klara and the product of the Durst family's dark rituals. Flint put the creature down permanently. The party's first true combat encounter in Barovia.

Klara's song was recovered from the keys of the harpsichord — a haunting mechanical clue to the orphanage's history.

~27th–28th Octyavr — Days 2–4 | Sessions 3–4
Sessions 3–4

Village of Barovia — First Encounter with Strahd

StrahdTravel
▼

The party travelled through the Svalich Woods, passing a body at the 7th tree and a horse corpse with saddle and rations. They arrived at the Village of Barovia and met Ismark at the Blood of the Vine Tavern. Over the following days, Strahd's carriage appeared on the road, Rahadin delivered a scroll on a phantom steed, and Strahd himself appeared — claiming Ireena as his own.

Ireena's cache was recovered: a moonstone gem brooch, yarrow flower wooden charm, and a tarnished bracelet bearing "May you find your path, and may you find light even in the darkest places."

~1st Neyavr — Days 7–8 | Sessions 5–10
Sessions 5–10

Tser Pool — Madam Eva's Tarokka Reading

LoreTravel
▼

The party escorted Ireena westward to the Tser Pool Vistani encampment, where Madam Eva had been expecting them. At midnight at the River Ylivis Cemetery, she delivered the full Tarokka reading:

  • Card 1 — The Traitor: History / Knowledge of the Ancients — "The lost soul will lead you to him" (ancient foe of a noble house)
  • Card 2 — The Myrmidon: Holy Symbol of Great Hope — wolf's den in the hills overlooking a mountain lake; treasure belongs to Mother Night
  • Card 3 — The Bishop: Weapon of Vengeance (Sunblade) — amber prison where the devil fears to tread; House of the Fallen Dragon leads the way
  • Card 4 — The Mists: Ally — a Vistana wanderer searching for her mentor; seek her at Saint Markovia's Abbey
  • Card 5 — The Marionette: Enemy's Location — Castle Ravenloft's heights; the beating heart of the castle

Madam Eva warned: "Be wise, don't believe everything you hear. Strahd received a reading this very morning. We were brought here so that we see the death march." Eliza (Vistani guard) joined the party.

~2nd Neyavr — Day 9 | Session 11
Session 11

The Bridge Knight — Argynvostholt Pointed

LoreTravel
▼

Travelling west from Tser Pool, the party met Ser Godfrey Guillem on the bridge over the Luna River — an oath-bound revenant of the Order of the Silver Dragon. He warned against werewolves to the west: "Head south from the walled town along the ash-grey path."

Vel found a broken silver pendant later identified as belonging to the Order of the Silver Dragon. A conspiracy of ravens began following the party.

✅ 3rd Neyavr — Sessions 12–13
Sessions 12–13

Arrive in Vallaki — Bones of St. Andral Stolen

TravelLore
▼

The party arrived at Vallaki (~1,200 people), passing Morgantha on the road carrying bundled figures in bags. Baron Vargas Vallakovich rules; Isek is the real power. Refugees from Barovia are camping outside, not permitted inside.

At the Blue Water Inn they met Rictavio (a disguised Van Richten), the Wachter brothers Nikolai and Karl, and wolf hunters Szoldar and Yvgeni.

At St. Andral's Church, Father Lucian revealed the sacred Bones of Saint Andral were stolen just after 9pm the previous night. The Festival of the Blazing Sun is on the 7th — if the bones are not returned, the Hallow spell fails. Vel agreed to recover them. Flint identified the silver dragon amulet as belonging to the Order of the Silver Dragon.

✅ 4th Neyavr — Sessions 14–19
Sessions 14–16

Coffin Maker's Shop — Vampire Nest & Bones Recovered

CombatLore
▼

Investigation led to Henrik Vandervoort's coffin shop where the bones were hidden in a wardrobe. An alarm triggered and vampires Ludmila and Anastasia confronted the party — "Skinny new plaything." The party fought their way out and recovered the bones, restoring divine protection to St. Andral's Church.

The Baron publicly humiliated Udo the shoemaker for refusing to attend the festival. Vel disguised as Isek to steal the Ireena doll from Blinksy's.

Session 17

Vistani Camp — Arabelle Missing, Yan Found Dead

Lore
▼

At the Vistani camp, the party met Luvash, father of Arabelle (whose 10th birthday it was). Arabelle had been kidnapped while her drunken guard Alexi slept — deliberately given wine by the traitor Yan, whose decapitated body was found near the Luna River alongside a mysterious signet ring.

Kasimir (a Dusk Elf with frostbitten hands from crossing Mt. Ghakis) possessed the ring, which bore Kazan's personal symbol — his tower sits on a causeway into Lake Baratok.

At the Baron's mansion, Victor revealed the spirit haunting the mirrors was Stella Wachter — her soul trapped in the Ethereal Plane during a teleportation experiment. She has one week left.

Session 19 — Evening

Dinner at Lady Fiona Wachter's — Plan to Kill Isek

Lore
▼

The party dined at Wachterhaus. Lady Fiona revealed Isek is the true power behind Baron Vargas Vallakovich (ruled 11 years; his father died suspiciously in his sleep at 50). Full moon is the 8th.

Fiona proposed a plan: lure Isek out with wine, poison him, release Udo's mother. In exchange: compensation and political change in Vallaki. The party freed Udo from a closet in the Baron's mansion. Ripley found Isek's attic lined with Ireena dolls.

5th Neyavr — Session 20
Session 20 — Early Morning

Vel Captured by Isek

Lore
▼

Vel was knocked out, gagged, and transported to Isek. Waking upside-down in front of him and four guards, Isek demanded: "Where is Ireena?" Vel gave up the church. Blinksy is suspected of having informed on the party. The party began planning their move to Lake Baratok and Kazan's Tower.

6th Neyavr — Sessions 21–22
Sessions 21–22

Kazan's Tower — Gargoyles, Van Richten Revealed, Arabelle Safe

CombatLoreTravel
▼

The party headed to Lake Baratok and Kazan's Tower. A barrel-topped wagon outside bore "Home of Esmeralda Davenir." The iron tower door radiated Abjuration magic. Gargoyles attacked as the party forced entry.

Inside: a colourful beaded bracelet around a silver dagger, the Tarokka card The Hooded One, and Van Richter's burned journal with a sketch of Rictavio. Three ceramic pots in the lily pads revealed Van Richter's child had been murdered by a vampire.

Back in Vallaki, Rictavio was confronted at Arasek's Stockyard and admitted the truth: he is Dr. Rudolph Van Richten. Arabelle was safely hidden in his wagon. Aragail agreed not to report to Strahd — granting the party one planted piece of misinformation to use whenever they choose. Van Richten advised healing Spiggot's lycanthropy before the full moon — wolfsbane in Krezk's blessed pool.

✅ 7th Neyavr — Festival of the Blazing Sun | Sessions 23–24
Sessions 23–24

⭐ Isek Killed at Lake Zarovich

CombatMilestone
▼

On the Festival of the Blazing Sun, the party tracked Isek through his afternoon rounds, following him as he drained a bottle of poisoned Red Dragon Crush wine (poisoned by Aragail). They cornered and killed him at Lake Zarovich.

Loot: Silvered Battle Axe, Amber Shard, 35gp, Studded Leather. The amber shard was later identified as a binding framework fragment — it only speaks when the bearer drops to zero hit points. Flint's shard was from the same source.

Isek's head was delivered to Wachterhaus. Blinksy had already fled the city. Note: Full moon falls on the 8th — one day later.

8th Neyavr — Full Moon (Recovery Day)
Full Moon

🌙 Full Moon — Spiggot's Lycanthropy Cured

LoreMilestone
▼

The full moon rose over Barovia. Mel cast Remove Curse on Spiggot, curing his lycanthropy. The party rested and prepared for the next phase — the Heartstone ritual to retrieve Stella Wachter, and plans to confront Morgantha.

9th Neyavr — Sessions 24–29
Sessions 26–27

⭐ Morgantha & the Nightmare Hags Defeated

CombatMilestone
▼

The party confronted Morgantha and her daughters at the Old Bonegrinder Mill. Flint delivered the killing blow with an Upspell Guiding Bolt critical hit and retrieved the Heartstone. Franz's children Josephia and Camel were found hidden in a bookcase upstairs.

The Heartstone is required (but not consumed) for the Ley Line ritual to retrieve Stella — the Wachterhaus cellar contains the necessary connection.

Session 27

Rahadin's Letter — Dinner Invitation to Castle Ravenloft

StrahdLore
▼

Walking back to the inn, Rahadin appeared with a letter: Strahd formally invited the party to dine at Castle Ravenloft in six days — the 14th of Neyavr. Safe travel to and from the castle promised.

Arabelle delivered a cryptic prophecy: "In darkness' night the light shall flee / no dawn to break with no reprieve / from the grave the dead shall rise (to be saved)."

Session 28

Mock Trial & Fiona's Cellar Ritual — Stella Retrieved

CombatLoreStrahdMilestone
▼

In the town square, Rahadin staged a mock trial; Henrik and Milovoj were found guilty (15 lashings and loss of hand respectively). Lady Fiona used the moment to consolidate power in Vallaki.

The party entered the Ethereal Plane via ritual in Fiona's cellar. Walls bore "In Silence, Death" and the word "Traitor." Strahd appeared briefly on his Nightmare steed during the ritual. Stella was successfully retrieved — confirmed by Viktor the following morning. The party gained a Spirit Mirror and Lantern of Revealing.

Session 29

⭐ The Sacred Tome of Strahd Recovered

CombatLoreMilestone
▼

Through the Ley Line and battle with Leo Delinzia (a Lich), the party uncovered the full history of Strahd's pact with the Dark Powers. Flint delivered the final blow and collected The Sacred Deep Crimson Red Tome of Strahd — Tarokka Card 1 resolved.

Key contents: Strahd's obsession with Tatyana, his pact with Death, his fear of the Sunsword, and his claim over the three Fanes. The final page bears a triangle inscribed "Exechanter."

Strahd's counter-verse (overheard by Vel): "The Sword lies in the hand of the Dead / The Ally walks in the Shadow / Cast a light on the Traitor and call him friend / They will trust the wrong soul, let them chase a ghost."

✅ 10th Neyavr — Sessions 30–33
Sessions 30–33

⭐ Wizard of Wines — Druids Defeated, Winery Cleansed

CombatTravelMilestone
▼

Erwin reported the winery was two days overdue. The party travelled south with Muriel. Druids had invaded three days prior, led by an Elder Druid wielding the Winter Splinter — a wand of petrified wood connected to the Gultiest tree at Yester Hill.

The Martikov family were sheltering in a hidden cavern. After clearing multiple floors of Needle, Vine, and Twig Blights plus four Druids, Spiggot destroyed the Winter Splinter wand. Every blight froze and crumbled. The Gultiest tree dissolved into grey sludge as cleansing rain washed the winery clean.

+2,000 XP. The Martikovs revealed they are were-ravens and members of the Keepers of the Feather.

✅ 11th Neyavr — Sessions 34–37
Session 34 — Morning

⭐ Strahd Kills Flint — The Amber Pact

CombatStrahdMilestoneLore
▼

Strahd appeared astride Beucephalus and paused upon seeing Flint: "Amber Temple has noticed you. I know it." Spiggot defied Strahd — and Flint was killed as punishment. Isek's amber shard began rotating around Flint's body.

Strahd offered: "You have seen enough to be useful. Die here normally — or accept the pact." Flint accepted the Amber Pact, selecting Isek's shard. He no longer has a heartbeat or breath. The ravens vowed to find a way to help.

Session 34 — Morning

Yester Hill — Ritual Combat; Ludmilla & the Ritualists

CombatLoreStrahd
▼

At the summit of Yester Hill, thirteen cloaked figures surrounded a stone fanged statue as druids and berserkers conducted a ritual to empower the Gultiest tree. Ludmilla — one of Strahd's vampire brides — led the dark-skinned mage (connected to Baba Lasaga) at the head of the ritual circle. The party fought through the ritualists to disrupt the ceremony. Strahd watched from atop his Nightmare, content to use the party as pawns.

Sessions 35–36

Kavan's Spirit, the Whispering Wall & Level Up

LoreStrahdMilestone
▼

The party sought the spirit of Kavan the Blood Drinker in the Slavich Woods — a druidic chieftain whose spear was stolen by the Forest Folk. He revealed the three winery gemstones are Gems of the Fanes — they must be brought to Soldau in the Balinoks, where two waterfalls meet, to restore the Ladies' power.

Ripley and Spiggot recovered the green gemstone from the Yester Hill statue before it detonated. Strahd met the party at the Whispering Wall — "the tomb of an ancient god" — congratulated them and vanished. Davian gave an obsidian whistle to Spiggot. Muriel revealed she overheard Strahd's personal Tarokka counter-reading.

Elric (Muriel's betrothed, Elvir's twin) was confirmed killed at Ravenloft as a were-raven. Muriel herself is a were-raven. +2,500 XP — Level Up.

Session 37 — Evening

Arrive in Krezk — Dimitri Krezkov, the Shrine

TravelLore
▼

The party arrived at Krezk — 20-foot walls, granted entry because of the wine delivery. They met Zelly (likely Esmerelda Davenir; olive-skinned Vistani, two different boots, one prosthetic leg) and Dimitri Krezkov, the Baron of Krezk.

The pool at the Shrine of the White Sun never freezes. Dimitri revealed the Krezkov family curse: lycanthropy manifests after a child turns 13. His daughter Kala (age 8) was killed by his son Ilya after a failed cure attempt. Ilya fled and went missing.

✅ 12th Neyavr — Sessions 38–39 | CURRENT
Session 38 — Morning

🌙 Long Rest — Esmerelda Confirmed in Krezk

LoreMilestone
▼

Long rest confirmed. Flint distributed new infusions: Spiggot (Radiant weapon), Mel (Mind Sharpener), Vel (Sonic Disruptor), Ripley (Recoil Dampener).

Esmerelda is in Krezk, living at the Abbey after being attacked by wolves 3–4 weeks ago. The party offered to find Ilya. Dimitri's sister Zeleeka — a werewolf unseen in Krezk for 30 years — is also connected. Ilya escaped on Octyavr 22.

Session 39 — CURRENT

📍 Inside the Abbey — Zelly, Ilya's Den Located

TravelLore
▼

The party entered Saint Markovia's Abbey. A Patchwork Golem guarded the east wing. Inside they found Zelly, who remembered Flint's workshop antics, Spiggot's stories, and Vel singing on a roof — but could not place Vel herself. She returned because Van Richten came back three months ago, and she felt something bizarre in the Abbey. The Abbott is growing increasingly unstable.

Through a pentagram chalk circle, contact was made with spirits. Ilya's location confirmed: a wolf den in a cavern at the spur of Mt. Garble — approximately 12 werewolves.
