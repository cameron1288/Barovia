The Domain of Barovia

1 Central Barovia
Castle Ravenloft

Strahd's home. Named after his mother Ravenovia. He sleeps far below it, among the dead beneath the stones — but will move to its heights when the party comes for him (Tarokka Card 5). His conquered wealth is buried beneath the belfry. Bats constantly fly toward it.

2 Eastern Barovia
Village of Barovia

The first village the party entered. Site of the undead assault and Kolyan's funeral. Father Donavich's church with Doru in the undercroft. The Blood of the Vine Tavern is the main gathering point. Kolyan found Ireena in the nearby Slavich Woods.

3 Central Barovia
Tser Pool Encampment

Vistani camp at Tser Pool, near Tser Falls. Madame Eva lives here. The Game of Stories and the full Tarokka reading happened here. Arturi, Slaromir, and Eliza are among the Vistani.

4 Central-Western Barovia
Vallaki

A walled town of roughly 1200 people. Now under Lady Fiona Wachter as Baronness. The old 'All will be well!' motto is no longer enforced. Founded over 500 years ago by Boris Valak. The Luna River runs nearby.

5 Vallaki
Blue Water Inn

Run by Erwin and Danika Martikov — both Keepers of the Feather. Destroyed by Isek's rampage, presumably repaired now he's dead.

6 Vallaki
Church of St. Andral

Father Lucian's church. The holy bones were stolen and recovered — divine protection restored. Ireena sheltered here safely after the wanted posters went up.

7 Vallaki
Wachter Estate

Lady Fiona Wachter's home, now the seat of Vallaki's Baronness. The cellar sits on a Layline — used for the Ethereal Plane ritual. Stella is here, recovered. A terrible creature reportedly lives in the cellar.

8 Vallaki
Baron's Mansion

Viktor's room has his teleportation circle and the mirror where Stella was visible for months. Isek's attic was lined with Ireena dolls. Library holds historical faction records. Udo was chained in a closet here.

9 Vallaki outskirts (north)
Lake Zarovich

Large dark lake north of Vallaki. Ireena recognises it from a dream. Bluto the drunk fisherman uses it. Szoldar and Yevgeni's shack is on its shore. Isek was killed here in Session 23.

10 Northwestern Barovia
Lake Baratok

Remote mountain lake in the northwest. Kazan's Tower sits on a causeway into it. Van Richter was based here. The hills above it are most likely where the Holy Symbol of Ravenkind (Tarokka Card 2) is hidden in a wolf's den.

11 Lake Baratok causeway
Kazan's Tower

Wizard Kazan's tower on a causeway into Lake Baratok. Iron door inscribed with his name, radiating Abjuration magic. Gargoyles guard it. Esmeralda Davenir's wagon was outside. Van Richter's journal and the Tarokka card The Hooded One were found here. Kazan's magic staff may still be inside.

12 Western Barovia
Wizards of Wine Winery

Davian Martikov's winery and the Keepers of the Feather economic base. Three stolen gemstones powered its magical grape-growing. Druids defeated — Winter Splinter destroyed in Session 33. Has a hidden trapdoor in nearby woods leading to a cavern below.

13 Southwestern Barovia
Yester Hill

Ancient hill sacred to the druids. The Gultiest tree is here. Strahd appeared at the top on his Nightmare horse. Thirteen cloaked figures formed a circle at the base. A stone statue with fangs stood at the centre. The whispering wall is at the base of the hill. May be connected to the Forest Fane.

14 Southwestern Barovia
Argynvostholt

Ruined mansion of the Order of the Silver Dragon. Directed here by both the spectral bridge knight and Tarokka Card 3. Find Ser Godfrey Gwilym. This is the 'House of the Fallen Dragon' — a required step before finding the Sunsword. South of the walled town on the ash-grey path.

15 Northwestern Barovia
Krezk / Saint Markovia's Abbey

Walled town past the Raven River to the northwest, protected by 20-foot-tall walls. Features a blessed pool at the Shrine of the White Sun. Saint Markovia's Abbey is located here, where screams and laughter echo from within; it is managed by the Abbott, an ageless figure who serves as a healer and creator of abominations. Zelly has been encountered here. The village has struggled with werewolf attacks and a family curse affecting the Baron's line. It serves as a focal point for the party's current investigation into the Fanes and the creation of a bride for Strahd.

16 Mountain region
The Amber Temple

Critical destination. Strahd confirmed it has 'noticed' Flint. This is where Strahd made his pact with the Dark Powers. The Sunsword (Tarokka Card 3) is very likely hidden here. Seriac/Norganas wants Flint here.

17 Southern Barovia (swamps)
Ruins of Berez

An old ghost-town in the southern swamps. Baba Lasaga is based here. She created the flying blights and stole the Red Dragon Crush gemstone.

18 Road to Vallaki
Old Bonegrinder Mill

Morganta's former home. Dream Pies were made here. Hags defeated. Franz's children found hidden upstairs. The party believes this and the 'Old Durst Mill' on the map are the same location referenced at different points in time.

19 Scattered across Barovia
The Three Fanes

Three ancient sacred sites pre-dating Strahd: Swamp Fane (Berez area), Forest Fane (Yester Hill area), Mountain Fane (Tsolenka Pass area). Strahd visited all three and claimed their power — 'thus I have become the Land.' Restoring them could break his connection to Barovia.

20 Other Plane
Ethereal Plane (Barovia layer)

Entered via ritual in Fiona's cellar in Session 28. Stella is recovered. Erasmus Van Richten (ghost) lives here. A Gallowspeaker and Night Hag also haunt this layer. A binding circle surrounds the Wachter Estate in this plane.

21 Eastern Barovia
Svalich Woods

Dense forest surrounding the eastern approach to Barovia. Kolyan found Ireena as a child in the Slavich Woods — likely the same area. The Durst children recognised it as familiar.

22 Village of Barovia
The Blood of the Vine Tavern

A popular tavern in the Village of Barovia, serving as a gathering place for adventurers and locals alike.

23 Tser Pool
Madame Eva's Wagon

The mobile home of the Vistani seer, Madame Eva, who provides guidance and prophecy to those who seek it. Last known location: Tser Pool Encampment.

24 Tser Pool
Tser Falls

A scenic waterfall near Tser Pool, often visited by travelers and adventurers seeking respite or information from the Vistani.

25 Vallaki outskirts (north)
Lake Zarovich

Large dark lake north of Vallaki. Ireena recognises it from a dream. Bluto the drunk fisherman uses it. Szoldar and Yevgeni's shack is on its shore. Isek was killed here in Session 23.

26 Lake Baratok
Esmeralda's Wagon

Esmeralda Davenir's rigged wagon, parked outside Kazan's Tower. Last known location: Lake Baratok.

27 Eastern Barovia
The Old Svalich Road

A treacherous road connecting various locations in Barovia, often patrolled by Strahd's minions and fraught with danger.

28 Throughout Barovia
The Svalich River

A winding river that flows through Barovia, providing a natural barrier and a source of life for the land, yet also a place of mystery and danger.
