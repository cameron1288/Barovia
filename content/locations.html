The Domain of Barovia

ID Location Name Region Description & Strategic Notes
1 Castle Ravenloft Central Barovia Strahd's home. Named after his mother Ravenovia. He sleeps far below it, among the dead beneath the stones — but will move to its heights when the party comes for him (Tarokka Card 5). His conquered wealth is buried beneath the belfry. Bats constantly fly toward it.
2 Village of Barovia Eastern Barovia The first village the party entered. Site of the undead assault and Kolyan's funeral. Father Donavich's church with Doru in the undercroft. The Blood of the Vine Tavern is the main gathering point. Kolyan found Ireena in the nearby Slavich Woods.
3 Tser Pool Encampment Central Barovia Vistani camp at Tser Pool, near Tser Falls. Madame Eva lives here. The Game of Stories and the full Tarokka reading happened here. Arturi, Slaromir, and Eliza are among the Vistani.
4 Vallaki Central-Western Barovia A walled town of roughly 1200 people. Now under Lady Fiona Wachter as Baronness. The old 'All will be well!' motto is no longer enforced. Founded over 500 years ago by Boris Valak. The Luna River runs nearby.
5 Blue Water Inn Vallaki Run by Erwin and Danika Martikov — both Keepers of the Feather. Destroyed by Isek's rampage, presumably repaired now he's dead.
6 Church of St. Andral Vallaki Father Lucian's church. The holy bones were stolen and recovered — divine protection restored. Ireena sheltered here safely after the wanted posters went up.
7 Wachter Estate Vallaki Lady Fiona Wachter's home, now the seat of Vallaki's Baronness. The cellar sits on a Layline — used for the Ethereal Plane ritual. Stella is here, recovered. A terrible creature reportedly lives in the cellar.
8 Baron's Mansion Vallaki Viktor's room has his teleportation circle and the mirror where Stella was visible for months. Isek's attic was lined with Ireena dolls. Library holds historical faction records. Udo was chained in a closet here.
9 Lake Zarovich Vallaki outskirts (north) Large dark lake north of Vallaki. Ireena recognises it from a dream. Bluto the drunk fisherman uses it. Szoldar and Yevgeni's shack is on its shore. Isek was killed here in Session 23.
10 Lake Baratok Northwestern Barovia Remote mountain lake in the northwest. Kazan's Tower sits on a causeway into it. Van Richter was based here. The hills above it are most likely where the Holy Symbol of Ravenkind (Tarokka Card 2) is hidden in a wolf's den.
11 Kazan's Tower Lake Baratok causeway Wizard Kazan's tower on a causeway into Lake Baratok. Iron door inscribed with his name, radiating Abjuration magic. Gargoyles guard it. Esmeralda Davenir's wagon was outside. Van Richter's journal and the Tarokka card The Hooded One were found here. Kazan's magic staff may still be inside.
12 Wizards of Wine Winery Western Barovia Davian Martikov's winery and the Keepers of the Feather economic base. Three stolen gemstones powered its magical grape-growing. Druids defeated — Winter Splinter destroyed in Session 33. Has a hidden trapdoor in nearby woods leading to a cavern below.
13 Yester Hill Southwestern Barovia Ancient hill sacred to the druids. The Gultiest tree is here. Strahd appeared at the top on his Nightmare horse. Thirteen cloaked figures formed a circle at the base. A stone statue with fangs stood at the centre. The whispering wall is at the base of the hill. May be connected to the Forest Fane.
14 Argynvostholt Southwestern Barovia Ruined mansion of the Order of the Silver Dragon. Directed here by both the spectral bridge knight and Tarokka Card 3. Find Ser Godfrey Gwilym. This is the 'House of the Fallen Dragon' — a required step before finding the Sunsword. South of the walled town on the ash-grey path.
15 Krezk / Saint Markovia's Abbey Northwestern Barovia Walled town past the Raven River to the northwest, protected by 20-foot-tall walls. Features a blessed pool at the Shrine of the White Sun. Saint Markovia’s Abbey is located here, where screams and laughter echo from within; it is managed by the Abbott, an ageless figure who serves as a healer and creator of abominations. Zelly has been encountered here. The village has struggled with werewolf attacks and a family curse affecting the Baron’s line. It serves as a focal point for the party's current investigation into the Fanes and the creation of a bride for Strahd.
16 The Amber Temple Mountain region Critical destination. Strahd confirmed it has 'noticed' Flint. This is where Strahd made his pact with the Dark Powers. The Sunsword (Tarokka Card 3) is very likely hidden here. Seriac/Norganas wants Flint here.
17 Ruins of Berez Southern Barovia (swamps) An old ghost-town in the southern swamps. Baba Lasaga is based here. She created the flying blights and stole the Red Dragon Crush gemstone.
18 Old Bonegrinder Mill Road to Vallaki Morganta's former home. Dream Pies were made here. Hags defeated. Franz's children found hidden upstairs. The party believes this and the 'Old Durst Mill' on the map are the same location referenced at different points in time.
19 The Three Fanes Scattered across Barovia Three ancient sacred sites pre-dating Strahd: Swamp Fane (Berez area), Forest Fane (Yester Hill area), Mountain Fane (Tsolenka Pass area). Strahd visited all three and claimed their power — 'thus I have become the Land.' Restoring them could break his connection to Barovia.
20 Ethereal Plane (Barovia layer) Other Plane Entered via ritual in Fiona's cellar in Session 28. Stella is recovered. Erasmus Van Richten (ghost) lives here. A Gallowspeaker and Night Hag also haunt this layer. A binding circle surrounds the Wachter Estate in this plane.
21 Svalich Woods Eastern Barovia Dense forest surrounding the eastern approach to Barovia. Kolyan found Ireena as a child in the Slavich Woods — likely the same area. The Durst children recognised it as familiar.
