The Adventuring Party

The souls bound by destiny to navigate the mists of Barovia.

Mel (Paul / Broken Blade)

Cleric — Healer

Description: The party's primary healer. His knife physically reacts whenever Rahadin is nearby. His sword is believed to have been forged by the same elvish tradition that made Rahadin's longsword, both once used in service to the Zarovich family. Mel has been researching the Sunsword and knows it was once wielded by Sergei Von Zarovich. Described by the "Dark Figure" at the Inn as - "Melda", The Quiet Watcher, Carries the weight of much blood, Silent Reckoner.

Notable Events: Led the investigation into the stolen St. Andral bones. Performed a ceremony after Isek was killed. Cured both Spiggot and Vel of their werewolf bites using Remove Curse and Greater Restoration. In the winery tunnels, stabbed himself with a runic wolf tooth to produce a blast of cold mist — a practical way to clear the yellow moss, nothing more. Discovered Isek's room full of Ireena dolls.

Velastra (Vel) (Jocelyn / Stone Crest)

Bard

Description: Quick-witted and resourceful with Disguise Self and Detect Thoughts. Found the broken silver dragon pendant. Abducted by Isek. She was bitten by a werewolf on the road to Vallaki and later cured by Mel's Greater Restoration. Described by the "Dark Figure" at the Inn as the Star That Fell Twice.

Notable Events: Won a brass spy glass in Arturi's Game of Stories at the Vistani camp — gave it to Flint who still carries it. Used Detect Thoughts to break the St. Andral investigation open. Successfully swapped Isek's wine with the poisoned decoy. Disguised herself as Isek to retrieve the Ireena doll from Blinsky. Asked Rictavio about Blue — he said 'His heart belongs to someone else. His tell is when his eyes flip upwards.' Received Arabelle's amulet of protection from Rictavio. Had a crucial vision during a trance — see Lore: Strahd's Counter-Reading.

Ripley (Jon / Half-one / Scarf)

Half Orc

Description: A wild card who repeatedly eats Dream Pies (addicted). He sensed something deeply wrong about Morganta before the group knew what she was. In Viktor's room, he saw a girl's face in the mirror — Spiggot looked immediately after and saw nothing. Described by the "Dark Figure" at the Inn as the "Masked Fang", wears shame as armor.

Notable Events: Found Udo the shoemaker chained in a closet in the Baron's mansion. Went upstairs alone with Morganta's daughter Bella at Old Bonegrinder Mill. When he attacked Udo's chains, the noise alerted a woman nearby. After Vel's abduction, he was the one who retraced her steps and found an abandoned house. Received a stuffed doll named Maud from Vel.

Spiggot (Jason / Feather)

Fighter / Warrior / Reborn

Description: Twice Born Exile — carries a memory of a past life on the battlefield. Bitten by a werewolf but fully cured by Mel. Declared opposition to Strahd without hesitation when asked by the spectral bridge knight. His defining moment: smashing the druid leader Loghoth's enchanted wand with his axe in Session 33. Every blight in the area froze and crumbled the instant it shattered. Described by the "Dark Figure" at the Inn as - "Mountain and Battlefield" two men at war with each other

Notable Events: Said 'We do not serve the lord' to the spectral bridge knight, committing the party. Bitten by werewolf, cured Session 27. Destroyed Wand Session 33 — 2000xp. Saw nothing when Ripley reported a girl's face in Viktor's mirror. Defied Strahd to his face which ended up killing his foster brother Flint.

Flint (Cameron / Echo of Gears / Amber Amulet)

Artificer / Inventor

Description: Pulled between his identity as an inventor and the growing influence of the entity inside his amber shard. He killed Walter the abomination in the Durst mansion — the act that pulled the group into Barovia. He carries the brass spy glass (from Vel) and the Sacred Tome of Strahd, collected from Leo Delirzan's vault in Session 29. Strahd personally acknowledged him at Yester Hill: 'Amber Temple has noticed you. I know it.' Described by the "Dark Figure" at the Inn as The Inventor with Inventions to Remember.

Notable Events: Accepted power from the shard entity during the wolf ambush (Session 12) and after he was killed by Strahd. Entity names: Norganas (Finger of Oblivion) and Seriac (Master of the Hunter). Its latest offer — Desperate Hunger — wants Flint to become a predator. Visible effects: blood turns black, left arm skin turning red with barbed spires. Collected the Sacred Tome (Card 1) in Session 29.
